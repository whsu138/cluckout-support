Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CluckOut ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information.

Information We Don't Collect

CluckOut is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data including:

  • Time entries
  • Wage calculations
  • Settings preferences

are stored locally on your device only.

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Core Data for time entries
  • UserDefaults for app settings

Notifications

If you enable reminders, these are scheduled locally and no data leaves your device.
