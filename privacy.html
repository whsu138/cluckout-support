Privacy Policy for CluckOut

Last updated: February 8, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CluckOut ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our app.

Information We Don't Collect

CluckOut is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics services
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Store any data on external servers

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Time entries (stored using Core Data)
  • Hourly rates and earnings calculations
  • App preferences and settings (stored using UserDefaults)
  • Reminder settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Data Export

When you use the export feature:

  • You control when and where to export your data
  • Exports are generated locally on your device
  • Data is exported in CSV format
  • You choose where to save or share the exported file

Notifications

If you enable reminders:

  • All notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  • No notification data is transmitted externally
  • You can manage notification permissions through your device settings

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete individual records or all data through the app
  • Remove the app to completely remove all associated data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
