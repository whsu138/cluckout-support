CluckOut Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Data Controller

CluckOut is the data controller for the processing described in this policy. For privacy requests and questions, contact us at hsudev138@gmail.com or via the support link in the App Store listing or the in-app support options.

Overview

CluckOut is a time tracking app. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. The app is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK); we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Legal Basis & Retention (EEA/UK)

For users in the European Economic Area and UK: we process your on-device data to provide the app under our contract with you; we process data for ads based on your consent (obtained via Google's consent tool where required). Your time-tracking data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Ad-related data retention is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Stored on Your Device

All your time entries, categories, reminders, and settings are stored only on your device using Apple's Core Data. We do not upload this data to our servers. The app works offline. You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app.

Categories of Personal Information

We do not collect personal information on our servers. On your device we process: time entries, categories, reminders, and app settings (stored locally). For advertising, our provider (Google AdMob) may receive device-related information (e.g. coarse location, device type) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We use this only for non-personalized ads. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for targeting.

Advertising

CluckOut shows banner ads provided by Google AdMob. We use non-personalized ads only (no personalized targeting based on your behavior). We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for ad targeting.

AdMob may collect device-related information (e.g. coarse location, device type) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent where required before showing ads.

Revoke Consent & Ad Choices

You can change or withdraw your consent for advertising at any time:

  • In the app: If we show a consent message (e.g. in EEA/UK), you can decline or change your choice when the message is shown again (e.g. after reinstall or app update).
  • On your device: On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and turn off “Allow Apps to Request to Track” or disable tracking for CluckOut. This limits cross-app tracking; CluckOut will still show non-personalized ads.

Because we use non-personalized ads only and do not use IDFA for targeting, revoking consent mainly affects optional personalized elements. You can also uninstall the app to stop all data processing by CluckOut.

Your Rights

Since we do not store your time-tracking data on our servers, that data is under your control on your device. You can export it (Settings → Record Management → Export) or delete it (Settings → Record Management → Clear Records) at any time.

EEA / UK (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and object to processing of your data; the right to data portability (we support this via export); the right to withdraw consent at any time (see Revoke Consent & Ad Choices); and the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority in your country.

US state privacy rights (e.g. California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah): Depending on your state, you may have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, to correct it, to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” (see below), to opt out of targeted advertising, and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. Use the in-app controls above or contact us via the support link in the App Store, in-app support, or hsudev138@gmail.com to exercise your rights.

Your California Privacy Choices / Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell your personal information. We use Google AdMob for non-personalized ads only and do not use IDFA for targeting. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising may still apply to some ad tech; we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

If you are a California resident and wish to exercise your right to know, delete, correct, or opt out of sale or sharing, use the in-app options (export/delete, ad consent) or contact us at hsudev138@gmail.com or via the support link in the App Store or in-app support. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
