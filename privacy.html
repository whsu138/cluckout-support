CluckOut Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

CluckOut is a time tracking app. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data Stored on Your Device

All your time entries, categories, reminders, and settings are stored only on your device using Apple’s Core Data. We do not upload this data to our servers. The app works offline. You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app.

Advertising

CluckOut shows banner ads provided by Google AdMob. We use non-personalized ads only (no personalized targeting based on your behavior). We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for ad targeting.

AdMob may collect device-related information (e.g. coarse location, device type) as described in Google’s Privacy Policy. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent where required before showing ads.

Revoke Consent & Ad Choices

You can change or withdraw your consent for advertising at any time:

  • In the app: If we show a consent message (e.g. in EEA/UK), you can decline or change your choice when the message is shown again (e.g. after reinstall or app update).
  • On your device: On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and turn off “Allow Apps to Request to Track” or disable tracking for CluckOut. This limits cross-app tracking; CluckOut will still show non-personalized ads.

Because we use non-personalized ads only and do not use IDFA for targeting, revoking consent mainly affects optional personalized elements. You can also uninstall the app to stop all data processing by CluckOut.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not store your data on our servers, your time-tracking data is under your control on your device. You can export it (Settings → Record Management → Export) or delete it (Settings → Record Management → Clear Records) at any time.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the support link in the App Store listing or the in-app support options.
