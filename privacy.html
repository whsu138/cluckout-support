Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

Introduction

CluckOut ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

CluckOut stores all your time tracking data locally on your device using Core Data. This includes:

  • Time entries (clock in/out times, durations)
  • Categories and customizations
  • Hourly rates and earnings calculations
  • Reminder settings
  • Export/import history

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

CluckOut uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information for ad serving purposes:

  • Device identifiers (such as device ID, but not linked to your identity)
  • Advertising identifiers (if available, but we use non-personalized ads)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

Important: We use non-personalized ads, which means:

  • Ad data is not linked to your personal identity
  • Ad data is not used to track you across apps or websites
  • Ad personalization is disabled

How We Use Your Information

Local Data

  • To provide time tracking functionality
  • To calculate earnings and generate summaries
  • To manage reminders and notifications
  • To enable data export/import features

Advertising Data

AdMob uses collected data solely for:

  • Displaying relevant banner advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud

This data is not used to build a profile about you or track you across different apps.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob for advertising. AdMob's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • User Messaging Platform (UMP): We use Google's UMP to manage consent for advertising in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

All your time tracking data is stored locally on your device using iOS Core Data. We do not transmit this data over the internet or store it on external servers. Your data remains under your control and can be exported or deleted at any time through the app's settings.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • You can view all your data within the app
  • You can export your data in CSV, Excel, or JSON formats
  • You can delete individual records or all records through the app settings
  • You can delete the app at any time, which removes all local data

Advertising Preferences

When you first launch the app, you may see a consent form for advertising. You can:

  • Accept or decline personalized advertising
  • Change your preferences later through iOS Settings

Note: CluckOut uses non-personalized ads by default, regardless of your consent choice.

Children's Privacy

CluckOut is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to external services.

International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your data is stored locally on your device and is not transferred internationally. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for users in the European Economic Area.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
