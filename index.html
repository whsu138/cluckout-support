Time Tracking Basics

How do I track my time?

1. Open CluckOut
2. Tap "Check In" when you start working
3. Tap "Check Out" when you finish

How do I edit a time entry?

1. Go to the Summary tab
2. Tap on the entry you want to edit
3. Modify the check-in/check-out times
4. Tap Save

Hourly Rates & Earnings

How do hourly rates work?

• Each time entry can have its own hourly rate
• Set your default rate in Settings
• Modify individual entry rates by editing the entry

How are earnings calculated?

• Earnings are calculated based on each entry's specific rate
• Active entries use your current default rate
• Completed entries maintain their historical rates

Data Management

How do I export my time records?

1. Go to Settings
2. Select "Records Management"
3. Tap "Export Records"

How do I import records?

1. Go to Settings > Records Management
2. Tap "Import History"
3. Select your CSV file

Note: CSV file should include check-in time, check-out time, and optionally hourly rate.

Notifications & Reminders

How do I set up reminders?

1. Go to Settings
2. Select "Reminders"
3. Enable reminders and set your preferred times
4. Allow notifications when prompted
