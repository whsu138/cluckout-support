Time Tracking Basics

How do I track my time?

From iPhone:
1. Open CluckOut
2. Tap "Clock In" when you start working
3. Tap "Clock Out" when you finish

From Apple Watch (New in 1.4.3):
1. Open the CluckOut app on your Apple Watch
2. Tap "Clock In" or "Clock Out" directly from your wrist
3. Your status syncs automatically with your iPhone

Enhanced in 1.4.0: Time tracking now includes meaningful animations with logo pulse effects, breathing button animations, and real-time duration updates with estimated pay display. Enjoy haptic feedback on supported devices for a more engaging experience.

New in 1.4.3: Full Apple Watch support allows you to track time from your wrist with real-time synchronization between devices. Perfect for quick clock in/out without reaching for your phone.

How do I edit a time entry?

1. Go to the Summary tab
2. Tap on the entry you want to edit
3. Modify the clock-in/clock-out times with second-level precision
4. Tap Save

Improved: Edit interface now supports more precise time adjustments and provides better validation.

How do I add a manual time entry?

1. Go to the Summary tab
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Enter clock-in and clock-out times with second-level precision
4. Set the hourly rate for this entry
5. Tap Save

Enhanced: Manual entry now supports more precise time input and better rate management.

5. Choose time period filtering (last 7/30/90 days, this month/year, custom periods)
6. Select categories to include in export

1. Go to the Summary tab
2. Choose your time frame (day, week, month, or year) and date using the date navigator
3. Optionally filter by category using the category selector
4. In the time records section, tap the "Print time records as PDF" button (printer icon)

2. Tap "Import History"
3. Select your file (CSV, Excel, or JSON)
4. Watch progress indicators during import

New in 1.4.2: Import now supports multiple file formats! The app automatically detects the format based on file extension (.csv, .xlsx, .xls, .json). Supports CSV files, Excel-compatible files with UTF-8 BOM handling, and JSON files with DataFrame format.

Enhanced in 1.4.0: Import includes progress indicators, enhanced error handling, and overnight shift support. Enjoy clean progress overlays and comprehensive validation.

Legacy Format Support: The app automatically detects and supports multiple time formats:

  • Overnight shifts: Properly handles records that span midnight

Note: Import files should include clock-in time, clock-out time, and optionally hourly rate. The app will automatically handle format detection, duplicate prevention, and overnight shift logic.

If notification permission is denied, the reminder toggle will be disabled and you'll see a warning message. To fix this:

What Apple Watch models are supported?

CluckOut supports Apple Watch Series 3 and later running watchOS 8.0 or later. The watch app requires a paired iPhone with the CluckOut app installed.

Why isn't my watch app connecting to my iPhone?

If you see a connection error on your watch app:

‚Ä¢ Make sure your Apple Watch is paired with your iPhone
‚Ä¢ Ensure both devices are nearby and Bluetooth is enabled
‚Ä¢ Try restarting both the watch app and iPhone app
‚Ä¢ Check that the CluckOut app is installed on your iPhone

The watch app requires an active Watch Connectivity session to communicate with your iPhone. The connection status indicator shows whether the session is active.

Can I use the watch app without my iPhone nearby?

The watch app requires an active connection to your iPhone via Watch Connectivity to clock in and out. While you can view the app interface, clock in/out operations require the iPhone to be nearby and connected via Bluetooth.
